# OfficeQA Skill

Answer questions grounded in office documents (PDFs, spreadsheets, presentations).
Targeted at U.S. Treasury Bulletin corpus for the Sentient Arena Cohort 0 competition.

## Capabilities

- Parse and extract content from PDFs, XLSX, PPTX, and DOCX files
- Locate relevant passages across multi-document corpora
- Perform quantitative reasoning over tabular data
- Synthesize answers with source citations
